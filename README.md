# Sales-performance
# 📱 Shop Mobile — Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing mobile phone sales performance across brands, models, payment methods, and regions in India — built to surface trends in revenue, transaction volume, and customer satisfaction.

## 📊 Overview

This dashboard provides a 360° view of mobile phone sales data, enabling stakeholders to filter and drill down by **month, mobile model, brand, payment method, and city** to uncover actionable sales insights.

### Key Metrics (KPIs)
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Total Sales | 769M |
| Transactions | 4K |
| Total Quantity Sold | 19K |
| Average Price | 40.11K |
| Customer Ratings (avg) | 3.69 / 5 |

## 🔍 Dashboard Features

- **Total Quantity by Month** — Line chart tracking unit sales trends across all 12 months, highlighting seasonal peaks and dips.
- **Total Sales by Mobile Model** — Bar chart comparing top-performing models (Vivo Y51, iPhone SE, OnePlus Nord, Galaxy Note 20, iPhone 11).
- **Transactions by Payment Method** — Pie chart breaking down purchases by UPI, Debit Card, Cash, and Credit Card.
- **Customer Ratings Distribution** — Funnel chart showing the spread of ratings from 1 to 5 stars.
- **Total Sales by Day** — Trend of sales performance across days of the week.
- **Geo Map View** — Sales distribution across Indian cities using bubble sizing by volume.
- **Brand Summary Table** — Comparative breakdown of Total Sales, Average Price, and Customer Ratings across Apple, OnePlus, Samsung, Vivo, and Xiaomi.
- **Interactive Slicers** — Filter by Month, Mobile Model, Payment Method, Brand, and City for dynamic exploration.

## 🏆 Key Insights

- **Apple** leads in total sales (₹602.29M) and holds the highest average price point (₹40,996.96) alongside the top customer rating (3.71).
- **Xiaomi**, despite the lowest average price, earns the highest customer satisfaction rating (3.72), suggesting strong value-for-money perception.
- Payment methods are fairly evenly distributed, with **UPI** slightly leading (27.14%) — reflecting India's growing digital payments adoption.
- Sales show a mild weekly decline from **Saturday to Thursday**, useful for planning promotions on higher-traffic days.
- The top 5 mobile models by sales are closely clustered (~0.20–0.22T each), indicating a competitive mid-to-premium segment.
